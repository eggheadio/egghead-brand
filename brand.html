House rules
  • ✓ one yolk button per shelf — the CTA earns it
  • ✓ active sits raised; open presses in — consistent physics
  • ✓ inputs are wells — you drop things in
  • ✓ paper stays paper, even at night — stickies, tape, index cards keep daylight colors
  • ✓ alternate card shelves with loose marginalia — never a wall of boxes
  • ✓ drawn, not rendered — hand-crafted warmth over gradient slop
  • ✓ the egg does not crack 🥚
